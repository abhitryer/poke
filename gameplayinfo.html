Information:

    

  • Damage dealt = [R * Atk + S * (Level-1) + B] or [R * SpAtk + S * (Level-1) + B]
    • 

  • Damage taken = [damage dealt * 600 / ( 600 + Def )] or [damage dealt * 600 / ( 600 + SpDef )]
    • 

  • Damage reduction = damage taken * (1 - F)
    • 

  • Where R is damage ratio
             S is the level multiplier
             B is base damage
             F is damage reduction ratio
    • 

  • Note that damage by attack and special attack are taken care by defense and special defense respectively.
    • 

  • Also there are no type weakness in this game.
    • 

  • Players need to gain points, level up their pokemon, take down objectives, take down opponents and score.
    • 

  • Different game modes have different maps and rules.
