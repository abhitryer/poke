  • X Speed: Increases speed for a short time

  • Slow Smoke: Slows down opponents in a set area

  • Goal Getter: Increases goal scoring speed for short time

  • Shedninja Doll: Makes it invincible for a short time, but the pokemon cannot move

  • Goal Hacker: Increases recovery effect in goal zones or decreases recovery effect in opponenes goal zones for a short time
